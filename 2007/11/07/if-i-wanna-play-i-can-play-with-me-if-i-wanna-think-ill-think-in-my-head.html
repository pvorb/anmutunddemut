Assoziativ zusammengetragene Gedanken, Wörter, Links, Mariella und eine Schlußfolgerung.

Es gilt: Weniger ist mehr. Immer. Immer. Ever ever ever ever ever. Wir müssen Webseiten wieder viel mehr so machen, dass sie uns gefallen! She got some prittstick and she glued her lips together. Web Design is 95% Typography and Don’t tell us busy pages look better! Mariella just crossed her arms
as she walked up the stairs and she went into her bedroom and she sat on her bed and she looked in the mirror. The tall website artistically considered. She marched to her wardrobe and threw away the colour because wearing black looks mysterious. Verschlichter Dich!. But Mariella just smiled as she skipped down the road because she knew all the secrets in her world. Dauerhaftigkeit ist eine ästhetische Kategorie! Yeah I'm never ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever gonna unglue my lips from being together

Daraus folgt, so wie die Nacht dem Tage: anmut und demut muss ligne clairesquer werden, nach alter Väter Sitte.
