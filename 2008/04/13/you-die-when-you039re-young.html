You die when you're young

Neon shines dim through desert skies and cities lies.
River link the wells of gas till they olverflow with
lightning seeds and secret secreets.
So let the twelve year old malt be my Bettwärme.
You die when you're young,
you die when you're young.
And the few steps home shall bring me Bettschwere.

Fußnoten

~ Artikel vom 13. 4. 2008

~ Ressort: Leben Lexikon: Bettschwere, Bettwärme, Elbe76, Hamburg
